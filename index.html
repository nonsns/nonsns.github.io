I am a Chief Expert at Huawei, director of the DataCom Lab in the Paris Research Center. I also lead a team of researchers passionate about using machine learning and artificial intelligence to solve hard networking problems. Feel free to look around for the team publications, as well as for the code and datasets we make available to the community.

Prior to joining Huawei, I occupied several positions in academia around the world, lately as a Chair Professor position at the INFRES department of Telecom ParisTech and a Professor at the DIX department of Ecole Polytechnique. You can find more information about my work in the cv section of this site (or in the short short or long PDF version (updated at most once per year).
